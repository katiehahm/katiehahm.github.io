Recognizing Hand-written Digits

Although reading numbers may be an easy task for humans, computers "read" in a very different way. For a project in CS221: Artificial Intelligence: Principles and Techniques at Stanford University, we used the multilayered perceptron, linear classifier, and autoencoder approaches to build an AI that recognizes single handwritten digits to 98.5% accuracy.

We used the MNIST dataset to train the AI using these various techniques and then tested them with the testing dataset to obtain our accuracy. The multilayered perceptron approach allowed the AI to learn the numbers most accurately.
