MicroFactory for Smart Manufacturing

During the summer of 2015, I also worked in collaboration with Stanford and SRI International on these micro robots. They have developed magnetically actuated microrobots that collaboratively build macro-scale high performance truss structures with carbon fiber rods.

I worked on developing the code for parsing 3D CAD structures into microrobotic controls with additional truss elements to improve their stability and stiffness.
